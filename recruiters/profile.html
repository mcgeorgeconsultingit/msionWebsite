About

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to unleash human potential. Since our inception in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, evolving into Nike in 1971, we have been driven by innovation and a passion for athletic excellence. Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world (*if you have a body, you are an athlete). Nike is more than a brand; it's a movement that connects athletes, communities, and the world through the love of sport. Our diverse range of products, from footwear to apparel and equipment, is designed to help athletes of all levels reach their full potential. We collaborate with the best athletes, coaches, and teams to constantly push the boundaries of performance and style.
Beyond our commitment to innovation, Nike is dedicated to sustainability and social responsibility. We strive to create positive impact in the communities where we operate, championing equality and inclusivity through initiatives that promote access to sport for all.
Join us in our journey to inspire and equip every athlete, from the elite competitor to the everyday enthusiast, to pursue their dreams and achieve greatness. Nike – Just Do It.
We’re committed to building a future where everyone — regardless of where they are from, the color of their skin, their abilities or who they love — can thrive.
